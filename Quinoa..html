2.Quinoa salad

 

This healthy version of a deli salad combines hearty quinoa and chickpeas with small ammounts of ham and mozzarella, so you get all of the flavor without overdoing it on the sodium. This healthy quinoa salad is great as dinner and doubles as a delicious lunch the next day.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups coarsely chopped Bibb,Boston,or butterhead lettuce.
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red bell pepper
  • 2 tabelspoons red- wine vinaigrette,divided (see Tip).
  • 1/4 cup cooked quinoa.
  • 1/2 cup canned low-sodium chickpeas,rinsed.
  • 1/4 cup drained canned artchoke hearts,qunaretered.
  • 1 slice low-sodium deli ham, diced(1 oz.)
  • 2 tabelspoonsshredded low- mositure,part- skim mozzarella chesse(1/2 oz.)

 

Directions

Step 1

Toss lettuce and pepper with 1 Tbsp.plus 1 tsp. vinaigrette and place on a 9-inch plate.

 Step 2

Toss quinoa and chickpeas with the remaining 2 tsp.vinaigrette and place on top of the lettuce and pepper.Top with artichokes,ham, and mozzarella.
