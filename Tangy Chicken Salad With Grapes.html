3.Tangy Chicken Salad With Grapes

 

Sweet red grapes and crunchy celery make this chicken salad sing, and also balance out the slight tang from the yogurt and lemon. But go ahead and customize the mix-ins with whatever you desire (perhaps some chopped nuts or dried(fruit) because this chicken salad recipe is easily adaptable. We use rotisserie chicken (look for unseasoned to keep sodium in check!) to simplify things, but any leftover cooked chicken will work                                                           .

 

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup canola mayonnasie
  • 1/2 cup plain whole -milk Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice (from 1 lemon)
  • 2 teaspoons dijon mustarrd
  • 1/2 teaspoons black peooer.
  • 5 cups choppes rotisserie chicken breast.
  • 1/2 cup decide celery.
  • 1 cup halved red seedless grapes.
  • 1/4 cup sliced scollions,divided.
  • 1 head butter lettuce

 

Directions 

step 1

stir together mayonnaise, yougurt,lemon juice,dijon and pepper in a  large bowl until combined.Add chicken,clerey,grapes and 3 tabelspoons scallions, and toss too combine serve on butter lettruce leaves and top with the remaining scallions.
