4.Cucumber Boursin Wrap

An easy lunch is only three ingredients away with thi Cucumber Borusin Wrap.Boursin spreadable cheese is flavorful and functional, as it holds  the chopped cucumber in place as you roll up the wrap.You can slice and enjoy this esasy sandwitch right away, or pack it up for lunch.if you are saving it for later,past the chopped cucumber with a paper towel to remove excess mositure.

Ingredients

  • 18-inch whole - wheat tortilla.
  • 3 tabelspoons Boursin cheese with garlic and herbs.
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped cucumber.

Directions

step 1

place tortilla on a clean work surface.Spread Boursin over the tortilla.Sprinkle evenly with cucumber.Roll up the tortilla and slice into 6 pieces.
